Iskra Galic

Iskra Galic defines herself as a Heartist—and we wholeheartedly agree! Our muse of the day is a young Australian painter, a bit of a magician. Thanks to her PhD in Psychology, Isra has gained access to many in need of healing, developing a unique partnership between conventional methods, energy work and artistic channelling.

From her hands emerge colorful canvases that place femininity at the center of her aesthetic. Iskra is like Cocteau making love to Picasso, or Matisse meeting Niki de Saint Phalle.

In her Brentwood studio, we stepped into the universe of this woman, for whom every canvas is destined to become a portal to healing. She develops her art not only to heal humans through a cathartic artistic process but also through the power of her own hands.

Tobias Churcher

Tobias Churcher moves within the equally select yet punk & queer sphere. This psychologist & marketing consultant, who heads the company Bendelta, hasn’t forgotten to be handsome.

While a sharp mind lies beneath the angelic face of this golden boy, our project « Caravan Melbourne » will mostly remember his sex-appeal & his versatile beauty. It was therefore impossible not to have him wear our Piece Unique, original XIXth century men uniform.

Joe

Joe is undeniably a handsome young man. He burst onto the scene like a gust of wind through the window of our Caravan Melbourne project. Joe is not just a model with unapologetically queer vibes. This aesthete & nature lover is the mastermind behind the Rtreet project, offering luxury retreats in the Australian bush.

Caravan Melbourne couldn’t miss the chance to meet this angelic figure who so perfectly embodies the spirit of our timeless Piece Unique.
