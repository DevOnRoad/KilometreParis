The House

Une maison
de travellers.

Born in Paris in 2015, Kilometre Paris is far more than a fashion brand. It is a permanent invitation to exploration. Our pieces are hand-embroidered by artisans from around the world — each stitch tells the story of a city, an encounter, a culture.

Oversized shirts, expedition dresses, parachute trousers, customisable Undercover bags... Our "Ready to Travel" collection is made for those who live with a suitcase in their eyes.
